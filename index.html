About Us

Welcome to Magic Mind Academy, Magic Mind Academy has been established in April 2010. In short Span of time Magic Mind Academy has gained wide popularity among the students, parents, institute and schools in India.

Magic Mind Academy has gained wide popularity among the children and parents world over, because of its unique advanced syllabus and play way method of teaching, coaching and training in a children friendly technique and style.

Magic Mind Academy devotes for edification of children in exploration of the human talents by educating on Brain Betterment on International Standard.
